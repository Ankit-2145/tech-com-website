Interview Preparation Websites

Back then, coding interviews were pretty straightforward, and interviewers would ask questions about basic algorithms, data structures, or languages.
Now, the situation for coding interviews has changed completely. In 2023, interviewers want you to have real-life experiences and unique problem-solving capabilities.
Their questions have also evolved to test specific abilities, such as pressure intake, out-of-the-box thinking, teamwork, soft skills, writing communication, organization skills, and working environment expertise.
In some cases, they skip programming questions totally and instead ask questions about collaboration or technology trends. So, like examiners, your approach to solving professional questions should also change.
Below, we will explore the best coding interview prep sites to prepare for any professional programming interviews.

  1. Codechef- Start your coding journey today
  2. HackerEarth- accurately assesses interview
  3. Coderbyte- improve your coding skills
  4. HackerRank- matching developers with great companies
  5. Pramp- practice mock interviews and coding problems
  6. AlgoExpert- ace the technical interviews
  7. LeetCode- a new way to learn coding
  8. Interview cake- programming interview questions+help
  9. Glassdoor- get interview questions and job
  10. freeCodeCamp- Learn to Code for FREE
  11. Big-O Cheat Sheet- Know Thy Complexities
  12. Codewars- achieve mastery through coding practice
  13. CodingBat- perfect for coding interview practices

Explore Master's Programs

After completing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), pursuing a master's degree opens up a world of opportunities for further specialization and career advancement in the field of computer science. A master's degree after BCA provides specialized knowledge and advanced skills in computer science. It opens doors to diverse career paths such as data science, AI, cybersecurity, and software engineering. Graduates can pursue roles as data scientists, software architects, cybersecurity analysts, and researchers, propelling their careers in the field.
So, here are some of the common master's programs you can pursue after completing BCA:

MCA

MCA (Masters in Computer Applications) is a postgraduate program that combines in-depth theoretical knowledge with practical application in computer science. It equips students with advanced skills in areas such as programming, software development, database management, and networking, preparing them for a wide range of IT and managerial roles. You can do MCA from the following Programs:

NIT(National Institute of Technology):

NIT conducts an entrance exam for getting seat in NIT called as NIMCET(National Institute of Technology Master of Computer Applications Common Entrance Test). NIT's offering MCA are as follows:

  • NIT Trichy - TamilNadu
  • NIT warngal - Telangana
  • NIT surathkal - Karnataka
  • NIT Allahabad - Uttar Pradesh
  • NIT Agartala -Tripura
  • NIT Bhopal - Madhya Pradesh.
  • NIT Durgapur - West Bengal
  • NIT Calicut - Kerala
  • NIT Raipur - Chhattisgarh
  • NIT Kurukshetra - Haryana
  • NIT Jamshedpur - Jharkhand
  • Central University of Hyderabad
CUET PG - Central university entrance test Post Graduate

It is an examination conducted for admission into various postgraduate programs offered by central universities. Universities offering MCA on the basis of CUET PG:

  • DU - Delhi University
  • JNU - Jawahar lal Nehru University
  • BHU - Bhartiya Hindu University
  • Pondicherry University
Universities offering MCA by their own entrance exam
  • Jamia Millia Islamia - Jamia Millia Islamia Entrance Exam MCA (JMI MCA)
  • Indraprasth University - Indraprasth University Common Entrance Test (IPU CET)
  • Pune University (Pune) - Maharashtra Masters of Computer Applications Common Entrance Test (MAH MCA CET)

  • LPU(Lovely Professional University) - Lovely Professional University National Entrance and Scholarship Test (LPU NEST)
  • BIT(Birla Institute of Technology) Mersa - BIT MCA

MBA

MBA (Master of Business Administration) is a prestigious degree that equips individuals with the skills and knowledge to excel in business and management. It offers a broad understanding of various business disciplines, leadership development, and strategic thinking. An MBA can enhance career opportunities, leading to managerial roles and entrepreneurial ventures.You can do MBA from the following Programs:

IIM(Indian Institute of Management)

IIM conducts CAT(Common Admission Test) and GMAT(Graduate Management Admission Test) is conducted by Graduate Management Admission Council for enrollment in IIM.Some of the IIM's are as follows:

  • IIM Ahmedabad (Gujarat)
  • IIM Bangalore (Bengaluru)
Private Universities

Some of the Private Universities offering MBA are as follows:

  • Symbiasis Institute of Business Management (Pune) - Symbiasis National Aptitude Test (SNAP)
  • Lovely Professional University (LPU) - LPUNEST /CAT / MAT / XAT / CMAT / NMAT
  • Amity University - LPUNEST /CAT / MAT / XAT / CMAT / NMAT /GMAT

MS

MS (Master of Science) degree provides specialized knowledge and advanced training in a specific field of study. It offers in-depth exploration of subjects like engineering, computer science, biology, or psychology.
